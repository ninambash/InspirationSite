Passion Fruit Tea

Passion fruit is a sweet, fruit with a delicious, slightly tart flavour. Because of its unique, intense, aromatic flavor characteristics,passion fruit is a “natural” ingredient for juice blends. There are 97 calories in 100 grams (3.5 ounces) of the passion fruit. Passion fruit is a good source of nutrients, especially fiber, vitamin A, vitamins B and vitamin C. The fruit is round and has a hard skin. It contains yellow pulp with black, edible seeds.For centuries, passion fruit is a summer fruit although it can be made at all seasons of the year.
